    6. Submit a bid as worker — confirm it appears in my-bids

    7. As worker: submit a bid on a client's job — client device should receive `bid_received` notification
    8. As client: accept a bid — worker device should receive `bid_accepted` notification
    9. As client: reject a different bid — worker device should receive `bid_rejected` notification (new)
    10. As client: advance job to InProgress — worker device should receive `job_in_progress` notification (new)
    11. As client: advance job to Completed — worker device should receive `job_completed` notification (new)
    12. Tap any received notification — confirm it opens the app and navigates to the correct screen
